# Quickstart

1. Open Daily Proof workspace.
2. Open Tasks.
3. Open an unassigned Todo task and confirm Next Action is Assign Employee.
4. Assign GPT Engineer.
5. Confirm Next Action is Start Work (placeholder).
6. Press Enter/Space and confirm status becomes In Progress.
7. Press Enter/Space and confirm status becomes Review with activity "Task moved to review".
8. Press Enter/Space and confirm status becomes Done with activity "Task marked done".
9. Confirm Done shows Completed and Enter/Space does not change the task.
10. Confirm GPT Engineer returns Idle if no other loaded local task is assigned.
