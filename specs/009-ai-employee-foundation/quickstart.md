# Quickstart: AI Employee Foundation

1. Open the Daily Proof office computer project portal.
2. Open Daily Proof, then the workspace, then Tasks.
3. Open a task detail and confirm Assigned is None before assignment.
4. Press Enter or Space to open Assign Employee.
5. Use Up/Down to select GPT Engineer and press Enter or Space.
6. Confirm task detail returns and shows Assigned: GPT Engineer.
7. Verify Esc navigation returns employee-selection to task-detail, task-list, workspace, project detail, project list, and closed portal.
